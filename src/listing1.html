Details & specifications

Upgrade your standard yellow car headlight bulbs with our xenon super white headlight bulbs to give your car headlights a distinct look that creates a day light like HID and heighten the sensation of your driving, enhancing road signs and road markings reflection.

Our xenon super white lamps are 100% filled with xenon gas.

This is a great replacement kit for your vehicle standard yellow halogen bulbs and they are 100% legal for road use, EU certified: E1, E3 & E4 & fully compliant with European ECE R37 Regulations.

Our product are made using Quartz Glass with UV-blocking technology to filter ultraviolet rays that are potentially damaging to modern plastic headlight units.

Our quality is trusted and guaranteed. Our products are all manufactured in the same factories where Philips and Osram bulbs are manufactured with the same automatic production technology.
